## Key Decision: α Interpretation

- **α defined as:** research opportunity rate — the probability that a researcher's programme generates a genuine finding worth reporting. A property of the research landscape, not the writing process.
- **Framing chosen:** Two-sided (Option C). AI could raise α (better tools help execution) or lower it (non-researchers enter the pool). Fixed α is the neutral case.
- **Key defence:** The collapse threshold a* = (c₀ − rλ₀)/(rμ + c₀φ) does not involve α. The tipping result is robust regardless of what AI does to research quality.
- **Concession:** Rising α would partially offset welfare damage after collapse but cannot prevent the collapse itself.
